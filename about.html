Hello! I'm Angelina, a developer, designer, and artist based in Seattle, WA

As a child, I always had a million different interests. Now, as an adult, I've realized that all those interests were connected by a common thread: I love building things to make people feel understood, delighted, and supported in their daily lives. Currently, I work at Chewy, making the everyday lives of veterinarians and clinic staff a little easier by creating features on a website that enables veterinarians to prescribe medications for their pet patients. Outside of that, you can find me gallery-hopping in Seattle (sometimes with my own art on display!) and taking long walks while listening to the next book on my to-read list.
